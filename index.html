Projects

Listed below are some of the most representative projects I've worked on.

CPW-fed Super Wideband Antenna
A Rogers RT/Duroid 5880 substrate CPW-fed super wideband antenna has been designed as a course requirement. The antenna working frequency range has been simulated using Ansys HFSS software from 4.68GHz to 110GHz which falls under C, X, Ku, K, Ka, Q, V, and W bands including mmWave.

Color Based Object Recognizer Robotic Arm
It is model of industrial robotic arm which can sense color and make decision of where to put an object of specific color.

Green Car
A system designed for reduction of the emission of CO2 from automobiles with the help of biological elements (Algae). The green tube is referring to the Algae storage where the filtered exhaust gas will be sent and Oxygen will be created by the photosynthesis which will then be provided in the engine for increasing efficiency.

Bluetooth Controlled Car
A bluetooth based Android controlled car model with gyroscopic control feature.

3D printer
A low cost 3D printer model from old computer DVD reader.
