Project Green Car
A system designed for reduction of the emition of CO2 from automobiles with the help of biological elements (Algae). The green tube is refering to the Algae storage where the filtered exhaust gase will be sent and Oxygen will be created by the photosynthesis which will then be provided in the engine for increasing efficiency.



Robotic Arm having Colorbased Object Detection Feature
It is model of industrial robotic arm which can sense color and make decision of where to put an object of specific color.



Image Classifier: Cats and Dogs

Efficient Fire Extinguisher with Emergency Exits
This idea is about stoping fire at the starting point. The fire extinguishers will be used directly in the circuit board where the fire actually broke down. The next part is about an alternative emergency spiral exit system around the building infrastructure.



IoT Based Smart Wheel Chair
A model of a Wheel chair designed for disabled person having the features of IoT based monitoring system of medical parameters (heartbeat, Blood pressure, tempurature) with GSM based location tracking system and alarm system for emergency health situation.
